Survival Guide

What I wish someone told me two years ago

The Golden Rules ⚡

  • Read the code - Even if AI wrote it, you own it
  • Start specific - "Build a button" beats "make it better"
  • Use the right model - Claude for coding, Gemini for planning
  • Version control everything - AI makes bold choices

The Reality Checks ⚠️

  • AI doesn't know your business logic (yet)
  • It will confidently rebuild what isn't broken
  • Sometimes it invents npm packages that should exist

"You don't need to be a software engineer.
You just need to be curious and willing to debug."
